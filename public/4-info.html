TEAM INFO

4th Grade League Info

Available leagues

  • Jeffco Conference: All 4th graders will play in the Jeffco Conference.
  • Gold Crown: Some 4th graders will also play in the Gold Crown league.

Jeffco Conference details

  • Info: The Jeffco Conference offers both competitive and recreational leagues.
  • Cost: $210
  • Uniform: $25 jersey if purchased from league. ~$100 if team chooses to purchase full uniform.
  • Season: Oct 30 - Feb 25
  • Practices: 1-2 practices per week.
  • Games: Games will begin the week of Nov 27 and can be any day of the week except Sunday.
Jeffco Conference

Gold Crown league details

  • Info: The Gold Crown league is more competitive than the Jeffco Conference and is intended for the more serious players. Gold Crown only offers competitive leagues.
  • Cost: ~$180 depending on the number of players on the team.
  • Uniform: Team will use the same uniform as purchased for Jeffco Conference.
  • Season: Jan 13 - Mar 4
  • Practices: Teams that play in both leagues may have up to 3 practices per week.
  • Games: Games will begin the week of Jan 13 and are typically played as a double header on Sunday.

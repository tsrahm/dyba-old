REGISTRATION

Welcome to DYBA Registration

Thanks for your interest in D'Evelyn Youth Basketball. Our registration is now being handled through Blue Sombrero. The annual registration fee is $30 per player. In order to register, you will be asked to take the following steps:

  1. Create a parent account on the Blue Sombrero website.
  2. Add all participants to your account.
  3. Register each participant for the appropriate program.

Please complete Team Placement registration by September 12th. We will accept registrations after the 12th, including on-site the day of Team Placement. However, we cannot guarantee t-shirt availability for registrations after the 12th.

Please click the Register button below to begin the process.
